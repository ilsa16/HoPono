Meet Evthoras

With a deep passion for holistic wellness and years of dedicated practice, Evthoras brings a unique blend of Eastern and Western massage traditions to every session at HoPono.

Trained in Indian Ayurveda, Chinese acupressure, Thai massage, deep tissue techniques, and traditional cupping therapy, Evthoras crafts each session to address the individual needs of every client.

“It is my pleasure to be a part of your journey towards wellness. Each person's body tells a unique story, and my role is to listen and respond with care.”

— Evthoras

Our Philosophy

"HoPono" draws inspiration from the Hawaiian concept of harmony and balance. We believe that true wellness comes from restoring equilibrium — between body and mind, tension and release, activity and rest.

Every treatment at our studio is designed not just to address physical tension, but to create a complete experience of tranquility. From the moment you walk in, you're stepping into a space dedicated entirely to your well-being.
